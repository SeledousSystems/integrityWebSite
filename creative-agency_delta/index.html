About Integrity

Integrity Software Consulting believe software is the most powerful, enabling, emboldening and influential engineering tool in the history of the world. But software is also the most complex and least understood; yet it is entwined in our lives, our businesses, our health and our lives.

We rely on software. Yet it is difficut to measure, unpredictable and its failure can be unexpected and devistating to life, mission or business.

Integrity's purpose is to simplify software and everything it touches. By helping you or your organisation understand your most important software systems, its strengths, weakness and oppertunities; you can assure they are safe, functional, reliable and maxmising their potential.

Whether you need to provide regulatory compliance, stakeholder assurance, supplier audit or risk assessment, Integrity can provide advice, audits, assessments, review or compliance for all your software and technology based systems. Integrity can support you across any critical or high integrity domian, including aerospace, health, military, transport, mining, emergency, financial and business critical systems.
