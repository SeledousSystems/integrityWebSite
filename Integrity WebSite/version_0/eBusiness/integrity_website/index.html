integrity's purpose


Software consultanting for citical systems

Integrity Software Consulting believe software is the most powerful, enabling, emboldening and influential engineering tool in the history of the world. But software is also the most complex and least understood yet it is entwined in our business, welfare, health and lives.

Our lives rely on software. Yet it is difficut to measure, unpredictable and its failure can be unexpected and devistating.

Integrity's purpose is to simplify software and everything it touches. By helping you or your organisation understand your most important software systems, its strengths weakness and oppertunities; you can ensure they are safe, functional, reliable and maxmising their potential.

Whether you need to provide regulatory comliance, stakeholder assurance, supplier audit or risk assessment, i can provide advice, audits, assessments and compliance for all your software and technology based systems. Integrity can support you across any domian, including aerospace, health, military, transport, mining, emergency, financial and business critical systems.

  • Safety assessments.
  • Software safety assessments.
  • Smart customer support.

Let us decode the complex software world and reduce your risk, save you time and money and ensure regulatory comliance.
